ScaleUp offers multiple flexible options for shared office spaces in Pune:

  • Daypass: For professionals needing a workspace for a single day
  • Weekly pass: Flexible solution for short-term projects
  • Hot Desk: Access to any available desk in our common area
  • Dedicated Desk: Your personal reserved workspace available 24/7
  • Private Cabins: Enclosed spaces for teams valuing privacy
  • Meeting Rooms: Professional spaces for meetings and presentations
  • Virtual Office: Business address and mail handling services

Each option includes amenities like high-speed WiFi, access to meeting rooms, and our community of professionals.
